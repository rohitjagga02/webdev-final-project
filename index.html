More quirky math applications and facts for you to satisfy yourself
Did you know that Ramanujan found a formula that approximates π more accurately than 22/7? Check out this calculator which uses his formula to calculate values of π.
Did you know that mathematicians have derived a version of the Pythagorean Theorem for right triangles on spherical surfaces? Check out the formula and an animation here.
The number 1.618 repeating is called the golden ratio, and it shows up everywhere in nature! From acorns, to seashells, to even the ratio of your body parts. Discover how your body obeys the golden ratio here.
